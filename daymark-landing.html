No servers. No sign-up. No one reading your data. Daymark works entirely from your device — online or off.

What's in v4.5

New in v4
◈

Custom categories

Create your own habit categories — Health, Work, Family, Finance — each with its own colour. Your habits, your structure.

New in v4
◉

Habit frequency

Set habits to daily, weekdays only, weekends, or a target like 3× a week. Daymark tracks the count and tells you where you stand.

New in v4
▦

Yearly heatmap

See your whole year at once — every day colour-coded by completion. Per-habit breakdowns show your all-time rate, current run, and best streak.

New in v4
◎

Weekly review

A Friday prompt to reflect on your week. What went well, what you want to change. Stored alongside your daily moments.

New in v4.5
📚

Reading log

Track every book you read — start date, finish date, notes. See how long each book took, your yearly count, and progress toward your reading goal.

New in v4.5
◐

Custom trackers

Log any number alongside your habits — weight, sleep hours, mood, calories. Up to 3 trackers, each with its own chart and optional baseline.

◻

Offline first

Installs like a native app. Works without internet. Your data lives in your browser — no account, no server, no subscription.

◬

Backup & export

Auto weekly backup to your Downloads. Export JSON or CSV any time. Import on a new device. Fully in your control.
